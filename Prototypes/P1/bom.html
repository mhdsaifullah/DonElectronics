— Common questions

BOM quoting — answered.

We accept Excel (.xlsx, .xls), CSV, and ODS. Your file needs at least a part number or MPN column, a quantity column, and a designator column. Our parser automatically detects common column headings (Part, MPN, Qty, Quantity, Ref, Designator, Description) — if your headings are unusual you can remap them after upload. There is no required template; just export directly from your CAD tool.
We query Farnell (UK), Mouser, Digikey, and RS Components in parallel via the Octopart API. We always select the supplier offering the best in-stock price at your required quantity. If a part is not in these catalogues, we flag it for manual sourcing — our team can usually find niche components through specialist distributors or direct from manufacturer. We also check our own internal inventory for parts from previous Synatel manufacturing runs.
When we detect a long lead time or zero stock, the system automatically searches for pin-compatible alternatives with equivalent specifications. You are shown the alternatives with their differences clearly noted (package change, frequency tolerance, etc.) and you select which to use. If no alternative is found automatically, our components engineer reviews the line manually and contacts you within 2 hours with options.
Yes. In step 3 (Assembly Options) you can link this BOM quote to an existing Bare PCB quote or create a new one. We then provide a single turn-key price for PCB fabrication plus component sourcing plus SMT and through-hole assembly. You can also supply your own boards if you only need assembly. Add a centroid (pick-and-place) file and we handle the rest. IPC-610 Class 2 or Class 3 workmanship available.
Yes. Select "Customer supply" in the Assembly Options step and we will provide an assembly-only price based on your BOM and component count. You ship your kitted parts to our Cannock facility. We also accept partial supply (combo) where you provide specific items — perhaps proprietary ICs or long-lead parts — and we source the rest. A shortage report is issued before manufacture starts.
By default we source RoHS-compliant components. If your design requires non-RoHS parts (common in some ATEX and high-temperature industrial applications), you can flag specific lines. Full material declarations and certificates of conformance are provided with every delivery. REACH compliance documentation is available on request.
