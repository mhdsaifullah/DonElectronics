— Common questions

Everything you need to know.

We accept RS-274X Gerber (industry standard), Excellon drill files, ODB++, and IPC-2581. Upload as a ZIP archive containing all layers. If you use Altium, KiCad, Eagle or Cadence, our DRC tool recognises the native export format automatically.
The instant quote is accurate to within ±5% for standard specs. Once our DFM team reviews your Gerbers (typically within 2 hours of upload), any adjustments for panelisation, special drill sizes or impedance requirements are reflected in the confirmed quote before you pay.
Yes. Upload your BOM and centroid file at step 3. We source components from Farnell, RS Components and Mouser, or you can supply your own. Turn-key assembly lead time is typically 5–10 working days. X-ray BGA inspection and conformal coating are available as add-ons.
Minimum quantity is 1 board. There is a £18 setup fee on all orders (included in the instant quote) which covers the DRC check, tooling and engineering review. For orders of 1–4 boards the setup cost dominates; larger runs benefit from better per-board economics.

We manufacture PCBs that go into ATEX-certified enclosures and assemblies.
